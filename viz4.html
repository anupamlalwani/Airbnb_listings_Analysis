Cancellation Policy

Cancellation policy is also an interesting factor when booking a Airbnb. A general thought would be if cancellation policy is moderate your listing might be full faster. But per pie chart, its seen that 41% of listings had strict cancellation policy. There are few charges apart from service charge in case of booking cancellation. There are also different categories of cancelltion like cancel before 30 days or 60 days. Both categories had very few counts; 7 and 17 respectively, so were removed for the simplicity of vizualization purpose.
