Listings Concentration by Neighborhood


This dashboard shows the concentration of airbnb listings by neighborhood and for a selected month/year. It also gives option to select an average price range when searching for listings in a neighborhood. It has much more information that it appears. When hovering over a specific listing, it shows the details like Neighborhood, Property Type, Bedrooms, Bathrooms and Avg Price for the listing.

This dashboard can be useful to understand and have a look at the overall spread of the listings by price in different neighborhoods of the city.
